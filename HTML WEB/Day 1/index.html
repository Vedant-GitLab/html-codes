*VIRAT KOHLI ~~

Virat Kohli is an iconic Indian cricketer, widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, he made his debut for India in 2008 and quickly established himself as a key player in all formats of the game. Known for his aggressive batting style, impeccable technique, and sharp cricketing mind, Kohli has broken numerous records, including the fastest to 8,000, 9,000, and 10,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs). His ability to chase down challenging targets and perform under pressure has earned him the nickname "Chase Master." As a former captain of India, Kohli led the team to several memorable victories, including a historic series win in Australia in 2021. Off the field, he is admired for his fitness, philanthropy, and outspoken nature.
